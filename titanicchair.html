Titanic Deck Chair

$1,100

The design of this deck chair closely follows the ones that were on the Titanic. I used 2” thick Cherry stock on these out of the center cuts of the milled logs. This provides a densely colored material. I used a special oil wax finish which provides for some durability but these chairs are not for direct exposure to the sun or rain. These chairs fold up for easy storage.


Dimensions: 32 1/4” height X 26 1/4”width X 64” length
